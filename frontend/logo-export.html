LinkMind Logo Export

How to Export

Right-click on any logo and select "Save image as..." or take a screenshot.

  • Favicon: Use 32x32 or 48x48
  • PWA icons: 192x192 and 512x512
  • iOS App Icon: 1024x1024 (use iOS style below)
  • Android Adaptive Icon: 512x512 or 1024x1024
